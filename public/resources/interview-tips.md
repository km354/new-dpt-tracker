# Interview Tips

## Preparation

### Research the Program
- Review the program's website thoroughly
- Understand their mission and values
- Know key faculty members and their research
- Prepare questions to ask interviewers

### Practice Common Questions
- Why do you want to pursue physical therapy?
- Why this program specifically?
- Tell us about yourself
- Describe a challenging situation and how you handled it
- What are your strengths and weaknesses?

## During the Interview

### Professional Appearance
- Dress professionally (business attire)
- Arrive early (10-15 minutes)
- Bring copies of your resume and portfolio
- Maintain good eye contact and posture

### Communication
- Speak clearly and confidently
- Listen actively to questions
- Use the STAR method for behavioral questions:
  - **S**ituation
  - **T**ask
  - **A**ction
  - **R**esult

## Follow-Up

- Send a thank-you email within 24-48 hours
- Mention specific points from your conversation
- Express continued interest in the program
